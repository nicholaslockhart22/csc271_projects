Chicken Parm Pasta

Chicken Parm Pasta is my favorite dish because it combines two of my favorite foods: crispy chicken parmesan and delicious pasta. The cheesy, saucy goodness makes it the ultimate comfort food.

Ingredients:

IngredientQuantity
Chicken breasts2
Breadcrumbs1 cup
Eggs2
Marinara sauce1 cup
Mozzarella cheese1 cup
Parmesan cheese1/2 cup
Pasta12 oz

Pad See Ew

Pad See Ew is my partner Meghan's favorite dish. It’s a Thai stir-fried noodle dish made with wide rice noodles, soy sauce, Chinese broccoli, eggs, and protein of choice. Meghan loves it because it’s flavorful, filling, and has a smoky taste from the wok.

Ingredients:

IngredientQuantity
Wide rice noodles8 oz
Soy sauce2 tbsp
Dark soy sauce1 tbsp
Garlic2 cloves
Chinese broccoli1 cup
Eggs2
Chicken, beef, or tofu6 oz
Vegetable oil1 tbsp
