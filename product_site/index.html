Welcome to Hair Apparent

In the City by the Sea, competition for the haircutting dollar can be fierce. Newport measures less than 12 square miles, but residents and visitors have more than 30 options when looking to get their hair done. Amid the choices, a neighborhood barbershop on Thames Street called Hair Apparent has built a reputation for friendly service, skilled barbers, and a laid-back atmosphere that keeps customers coming back. By combining traditional barbershop values with the convenience of modern appointment scheduling, Hair Apparent has carved out its own space in a crowded market. At Hair Apparent, we provide professional cuts, fresh fades, and excellent service. Our goal is to make your visit quick, easy, and enjoyable. No more long waits or unorganized walk-ins!

Why Choose Hair Apparent?

Our website helps solve the common barbershop problem of waiting in line. You can book online or call us directly so you’ll always know when it’s your turn in the chair.
